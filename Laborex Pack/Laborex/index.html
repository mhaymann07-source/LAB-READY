Quick Lab Tips

Did You Know?

🧪

Always Add Acid to Water

When diluting acids, always pour acid into water (not water into acid) to prevent violent splashing.

🔥

Know Your Fire Extinguisher

Class B extinguishers (CO₂ or dry chemical) for flammable liquids. Never use water on chemical fires.

👓

Safety Goggles Always

Even for "simple" experiments – protect your eyes from chemical splashes and broken glass.
